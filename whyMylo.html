Event Management

We know that events come in all sizes ranging from lunch and learns to webinars and large gatherings. MyloTEAM’s highly flexible event management platform allows you to make sure all events are planned and executed with ease even as your business grows and expands.

Event Administration - Before, During and After

Events are not a linear experience and in an ideal world there is a before, during and after that unlock hidden potential from information captured from your events. MyloTEAM’s platform has vast functionality and integrates deeply with Salesforce to handle your client experience. Create role based users so support staff have what they need without sifting through all the data. Attendees may have different value to you, so go ahead, create ticket registrations of all types and market to your invitees using promo codes and discounts. Want to see if an invitation has been opened or if the invitee is registered? Our Salesforce integration shows you where things are in the moment. It’s the day of your event. Shut down long check in lines and check attendees in using MyloTEAM’s Smartphone scanner, then hand them a custom branded badge. Send a custom report to the caterer and leaders of breakout sessions. Now loop back and run reports to see information without using unwieldy manual spreadsheets.

Make Attendees Your Life-Long Customers

We are committed to the belief that attendees are people not transactions. So, you should be able to see that Susie comes to your monthly meetups and annual conference. Whereas, John went to 6 webinars, 3 lunch and learns and also attends the annual conference. Now you know their interests, how they like to engage and you can market to them in a way that matters to them with your promotions. See it in MyloTEAM or track it in Salesforce or both.

Create a TEAM with Social World

Conversations are king but in business, privacy and focus are critical. Twitter and Facebook are not ideal. Social World allows you to not only create a private social network, but you can create multiple ones based on group interest to support a single event. Now attendees can interact with each other before the event, during it even if they are in their room after hours with that one last thought, or spring boarding ideas to one another weeks later.

Use Case: Association Annual International Gathering

It’s the first ever annual event for global professional association and attendees are flying in from all over the world. Many of the members had participated in events over the year, including local lunch and learns hosted by their partners, as well as monthly meetups and webinars. Success dictated whether there would be a year two and information on each attendee was critical to create a personalized event without too much time overhead. The event managment team used MyloTEAM to look at each attendee’s event history, as people, and offer various registration types based on membership as well as create breakouts based on common interests over attendee history. All the attendee registration information, including event type, dietary needs and breakout placement was available, up to the moment, when attendees arrived and picked up their badge, also created in the MyloTEAM solution. Registration was far better than expected so the event staff used the MyloTEAM waitlist feature to ensure that the event had no empty seats and those waitlisted were informed that a seat was open now. The day of the Using MyloTEAM event management software, reports were run relevant to the support staff’s task. Support staff sent role based reports, via a link, to caterers for dietary information, and breakout leaders to see who signed up for which session. When two of the breakout rooms became unavailable at the last minute, event managers emailed appropriate attendees to direct them to the new rooms. Better yet the attendees were able to use Social World, a private social network, to connect with each other in unique groups set up for their interest area and could communicate with each other.

Training Registration

Education is on the go just like students. Why would you have three different training & class registration systems? MyloTEAM puts your course management in one place supporting a student’s class registration whether it’s a leader led class, a classroom with an instructor or an on-demand recorded session.

Different Students Have DIfferent Value

One of the main things any organization needs, in offering class registration, is the ability to offer different bundles, price points, and promotions. MyloTEAM allows you to target students as individuals, based on how you want to engage them, with an easy system that allows different price points and promotions, be it member based or time based.

Transactions Under Control, in Real Time

Taking payments in real time, and being able to see where the money is going and who paid is critical. It allows you to see where your revenues are coming from, who is contributing, and how things are breaking out.

Use Case - Learning Organization on the Go

Like so many other global professional organizations the is one had a disparate member base, each with unique needs, but they all had the same desires to complete quarterly and annual classes. The headquarters needed to be able to see where the classes were being taken and how. Previously they had used a online event system for the classes and had no idea, without use of many spreadsheets, how class registrations were unfolding, It took three admins who shared various document across the globe and in many cases the classes were losing money because they could not forecast. MyloTEAM allowed the organization to plan a training curriculum that supported live classes for the bigger cities, online live classes for people who lived in the same region as their instructors, and on-demand classes, recorded previously, for some of their outlying members in time zones that matched. They were able to give time based discounts to increase early registrations and promotions for students who were enrolling in follow on classes. Students had the option of Visa, Master Card or even PayPal to enroll, and the organization could track each day where revenues were flowing from. The end of each class cycle allowed them to see the increasing trend for on-demand classes even in their busy cities. Through changes to training offerings, based on this data, they saw an increase in revenues by 20% and class attendance completion of 40%.

Certification Management

Education no longer stops with a four year degree, and in this world of increasing education costs, certification is becoming a critical part of ensuring that organizations know where their students stand in education. This spans from healthcare, where people’s lives depend on certified health workers, to technology where students are pursuing technical certification as a means of entering the job market faster or proving they have skills to pivot in their career.

Certification Management by One System

Recent studies show that in many cases certification is run manually, by one person, who has a central job that is not certification management. They have to remember to check into spreadsheets, or member databases, to see who is up for certification renewal or where particular students are in the certification process. MyloTEAM allows you to easily see, through custom reports, who is due to recertify or where they are in the process as individuals or based on time. Email them with their deadlines and requirements all from one place. Plus using class registration capabilities you can get them registered for necessary classes.

Certificates Verify Skills

Today it’s not enough to just state your claim on your skills and experience, and in many industries being hired or offering services is dependant on being able to validate one’s skills. Rising crisis management in healthcare and in disasters need to have employees who are validated officially by certificate. MyloTEAM not only lets you use certificate management of your employees and members, but gives you tools to print out or share certification from anywhere. Use class management to see where individuals are in the process.
