Our Team


Micahel McNally

CO-FOUNDER & CEO

Michael McNally has been President and CEO of Mylo Solutions since its inception in September, 2013. Under his leadership, Mylo is proving to be a disruptive catalyst for creativity and ingenuity in the training management industry. Mylo has been generated revenue since launch and is developing creative automated training management solutions for key industry verticals that are clamoring for evolving changes in training. Today, Michael is guiding Mylo to lead the charge in driving the global implementation of training management to create a more informed business environment with all of the clients' various stakeholders – including employees, partners, and customers. Michael has extensive experience building some of the world's most recognized brands at companies like Citi, MasterCard and Visa, as well as innovating new market strategies, and increasing the lifetime customer value for companies in a variety of industries. A proven leader and an experienced entrepreneur with a unique understanding of the SaaS market, he co-founded three SaaS based services companies. All three were successfully exited with either IPOs or as strategic acquisitions.

Sunil Maulik

CHIEF STRATEGY OFFICER

Sunil Maulik has over twenty years of sales and business development experience in Silicon Valley. He has co-founded three companies (PeoplePower, an energy analytics company; 500Friends, a social e-commerce analytics company; and GeneEd, a provider of healthcare e-learning). In addition, he has held senior sales and management positions at Pangea Systems, Tripos, Hoffmann-La Roche, Oxford Molecular and IntelliGenetics. Sunil's book "Molecular Biotechnology" (J. Wiley & Sons), co-authored with Dr. Salil D. Patel, sold over 4000 copies and has been used in four university courses. He has worked in antibody engineering at Oxford Molecular (LSE: OXMOL), molecular modeling at Tripos(NASDAQ:TRPS), and bioinformatics at Hoffman-La Roche (ROCHE). He started and ran his own eHealth company (GeneEd) from 1999-2007. He is on the advisory boards of Health2.0 and Health Technology Forum and is a speaker at Quantified Self, Habit Design and U.C. Berkeley. He holds patents in gene pattern-matching, e-learning and e-mail analytics. Sunil was winner (in conjunction with John Teeter and Gene Wang) of a $1.45MM SBIR grant from the DoE (2009), and with Banny Banerjee of the Stanford d-school, a GE "Ecomagination" award (2010). Sunil did his undergraduate work in Physics and Biology with Nobel prize-winner Maurice Wilkins. His Ph.D. is in Biophysics where he worked with Nobel prize-winner Aaron Klug and Prof. D.L.D. Caspar. (His research on mathematical methods for 3-D image reconstruction was published on the cover of the journal Nature.) Sunil currently runs workshops on designing products for long-term behavior change.

Bill Briner

CO-FOUNDER & V.P. PRODUCT AND SALES

Bill Briner has been in the Software-as-a-Service service business since 2000, and is recognized as a pioneer and thought-leader in enterprise software application development and integration. It's fair to say that he was one of the first to bring Training to the Cloud, and conceived of, architected and managed the development of the MyloTEAM solution. The product architecture he designed for Mylo is the culmination of that experience; it's an enterprise-class Training and Marketing Events administration and management solution that can be adapted to any industry or vertical. Prior to Mylo, Bill was the VP of Products at Web2Web Marketing. Before joining Web2Web Marketing, he successfully managed and launched several enterprise products in the EAI and Services Sourcing and E-Procurement areas. He has been involved in several start-ups, such as CAI, CascadeWorks, and Elance. Bill holds an M.S. from the University of Nevada-Reno's Mackay School of Mines and worked as a professional geologist for many years before joining the high tech world. Bill is an avid motorcyclist and also holds a USCG Charter Boat Captain license. He loves to head out to sea – or to the open road - to help him think.

Dave Ghosh

V.P. GLOBAL SALES & BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

Dave Ghosh is responsible for Mylo'sglobal sales and business development and building the sales team and channels to bring the MyloTEAM cloud-based Training and Events management solution to clients and partners across the globe. Dave has over two decades of experience managing sales for high-tech startups and later-stage companies. Prior to joining Dave was the VP of Enterprise Sales at Web2Web Marketing where he successfully expanded the decade old SMB Event Management client base to the Enterprise market. Between 2001 and 2009, Dave was a limited partner at Xinify and his contributions as the chief sales officer took the IT Governance/PPM tools and Services Company to finally the technology being acquired by Computer Associates. His first self-funded startup in 1995, Ravel offered an automated Y2K correction product and under his sales leadership the firm's revenue grew from zero to $30M+ in three years and was finally acquired by Oracle. For his outstanding success in inorganic growth and channel sales, Dave was profiled in the Top 50 CEOs by the Reseller Magazine, a publication of the Ziff Davis group. Although Dave has an engineering education, Dave started his career as a journalist, helping to establish PC World as a major publication in India.
